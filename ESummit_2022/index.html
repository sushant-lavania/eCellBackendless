What is ESummit?

This April sees us return with our annual Entrepreneurship Summit, a three-day conclave all about entrepreneurship and propagating its idea further into today's youth that is already keen to innovate for the greater good. Since its advent, E-Summit has been an epicentre of eminence embodied in ideas as relevant as the sun.

It has always been achieved by events that resonate deeply with participants and provide them with a platform to be vocal about quandaries they feel need due addressal. This time around, we provide them a pedestal in the form of eight events intricately constructed to test their wit and even provide them with some much-needed relief.
